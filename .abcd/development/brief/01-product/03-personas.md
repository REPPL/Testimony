# Personas

- **Alice** — the product-thinking maintainer. She tests her own applications
  in Mode A: runs a session against a dev build, thinks aloud, then reads the
  report to turn moments of friction into issues anchored in the code. She is
  also the pipeline's first and most frequent user.
- **Bob** — a participant. He works through Alice's task list while thinking
  aloud and is otherwise not a user of the tool at all: his voice and screen
  stay on the capture machine, and he appears in derived artefacts only as a
  pseudonym (`P1`, `P2`, …).
- **Carol** — a design researcher working in Mode B. She demos third-party
  applications, narrating what she likes and dislikes, to build a tagged
  pattern library that feeds design decisions — evidence-backed "make it feel
  like this" references, each with a quote and a keyframe.
